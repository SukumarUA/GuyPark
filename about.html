Our story

A community pharmacy with the time to care.

Guy Park Pharmacy was built on a simple idea: prescription care should feel personal. We're independent, which means we make our own decisions about how to spend time with patients, how long to keep someone on the phone, and whether to chase down a prior authorization that another pharmacy would just hand back.

We answer our phones. We know your kids' names. We remember which medication gave you a stomachache last year. And if you're not sure whether you can afford something, we'll look for a way to make it work before we put it in your bag.

That's not a tagline, it's how we run the place every day.

Why independent matters

Three things you'll feel right away.

You'll talk to a person

No phone trees, no offshore call centers. When you call us, a member of our team picks up, usually within a couple of rings.

You'll spend less

We hunt down coupons, foundation grants, and cash-price alternatives. We've saved patients hundreds of dollars on a single fill, for free.

You'll stay on track

Med synchronization, blister packs, and refill reminders mean fewer missed doses and fewer "I ran out yesterday" emergencies.
