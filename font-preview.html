My recommendation

For a women-owned, independent community pharmacy with a bold red/blue logo, my pick is Option 6: Fraunces + Inter. Reasoning:

· Fraunces in the headlines adds warmth and trust, exactly the "people who know your name" feeling, without going traditional or stuffy.

· Inter for body stays modern and effortless to read, especially for older patients.

· The bold cobalt + red brand carries the modern energy; the serif adds the human warmth so the whole thing doesn't feel like a SaaS startup.

· If you'd rather stick to one font, Option 2 (DM Sans) is the strongest single-font pick, warmer than Inter, modern, and friendly.
