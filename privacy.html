Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect information on this website.

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy covers information collected through this website only. How we handle your protected health information (PHI) as a pharmacy is described in our separate Notice of Privacy Practices, as required by HIPAA.

Guy Park Pharmacy LLC ("Guy Park Pharmacy," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect from visitors to guyparkpharmacy.com, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using this website, you agree to the practices described below.

Information we collect

Information you give us

When you fill out our contact form, request a callback, or otherwise reach out through this website, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • Your email address (optional)
  • The type of inquiry you've selected
  • The contents of any message you send us

We ask you not to include prescription numbers, medication names, dosages, or other protected health information in our contact form. Web forms are not a secure channel for PHI. For anything Rx-related, please call us at (518) 212-6020.

Information collected automatically

When you visit our website, our hosting provider may automatically log standard technical information including your IP address, browser type, the page you came from, the pages you viewed, and the date and time of your visit. This information is used to keep the site running, prevent abuse, and understand which pages are most useful to visitors.

Cookies

This website uses only essential cookies required for basic functionality (such as remembering whether you've closed a notification). We do not currently use third-party advertising cookies or behavioral-tracking cookies on this site. If we add analytics or other tracking in the future, we will update this policy.

How we use the information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your question or request
  • Schedule a callback or follow-up
  • Improve the content and usability of our website
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations

How we share information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information only:

  • With service providers who host or maintain our website, under confidentiality obligations
  • If required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our patients, our team, or the public
  • With your written permission

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect the information collected through this website. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure. Please do not transmit sensitive medical or financial information through this site, call us instead.

Children's privacy

This website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information through this site, please contact us so we can delete it.

Links to other websites

Our website may link to third-party websites (for example, Google Maps, Facebook, or insurance providers). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Please review their policies before sharing information.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Significant changes will be posted prominently on the website.
