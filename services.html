Services that work the way you do.

We've built our practice around the things patients tell us are the hardest about managing medication, insurance, cost, refills, side effects, and just getting it all to the front door. Here's how we help.

01, Everyday care

Prescription Refills & Transfers

Whether you're refilling a long-time prescription or moving your meds over from another pharmacy, we make it effortless. Request a refill by phone or through our website, and if you're transferring, we'll coordinate directly with your old pharmacy so you don't run out of doses in the meantime.

  • Refill by phone, in-store, or via our online request form
  • We contact your previous pharmacy on your behalf for transfers
  • Automatic refill reminders so nothing falls through the cracks
  • 90-day supplies available where your insurance allows
02, Bring it to you

Free Local Delivery

If you can't make it in, we'll come to you, at no cost. Our delivery service covers Amsterdam and surrounding communities, and we're happy to coordinate with caregivers and family members so the right person receives the package.

  • Free delivery in Amsterdam and nearby Mohawk Valley towns
  • No minimum order, no surcharges, no membership fee
  • Same-day delivery when prescriptions are called in early
  • Contactless drop-off available on request
03, Stay protected

Vaccines & Immunizations

Get your seasonal and routine vaccines right at the counter, administered by our pharmacist, no appointment needed for most. We bill your insurance directly when covered.

  • Seasonal flu (every fall)
  • COVID-19 boosters as they become available
  • Shingles (Shingrix), 2-dose series
  • Pneumonia (Prevnar, Pneumovax)
  • Tdap, tetanus, hepatitis A & B
  • Travel-related vaccines on request
04, Stay on track

Medication Adherence, Med Sync & Blister Packs

Managing five, ten, or fifteen prescriptions a month is a lot. Our adherence program coordinates all of your refill dates so you pick up everything in one trip, and for patients with complex regimens, we offer blister packs that group every dose by day and time so nothing gets missed.

  • Medication synchronization, one monthly pickup, everything aligned
  • Blister-pack packaging for daily/weekly schedules
  • Refill reminders by phone, text, or email
  • Especially popular with seniors and family caregivers
05, Real conversations

Patient Education & Counseling

If you have a question about your medication, we want you to ask it. Our pharmacist sits down with patients to explain what each prescription is for, how to take it, what side effects to watch for, and how it interacts with everything else you're taking, in plain language, not pharmacy-speak.

  • New-prescription consultations, every time
  • Drug-interaction reviews when something is added
  • Side-effect coaching so you know what's normal and what isn't
  • Translation help available, our team speaks English, Spanish, and Italian
06, We handle insurance

Prior Authorization Assistance

When your insurance kicks back a "prior authorization required" notice, getting that approved typically means a lot of phone calls between your prescriber, your insurer, and the pharmacy. We take that off your plate, we'll talk to everyone and push the paperwork through so your medication gets covered as fast as possible.

  • We initiate the PA request directly with your prescriber's office
  • We follow up with your insurer until a decision is reached
  • We keep you in the loop with status updates
  • If a PA is denied, we work on appeals or alternatives
07, Make it affordable

Co-Pay Assistance & Manufacturer Coupons

If a medication price comes back higher than you expect, don't walk away, talk to us. We actively search for manufacturer coupons, patient-assistance foundations, and discount programs that can take expensive prescriptions from "not affordable" to "yes, fill it." There's no extra charge for this service.

  • Manufacturer co-pay cards for brand-name medications
  • Foundation grants for specialty therapies
  • Discount-program enrollment (GoodRx, etc.) when it saves you money
  • Cash-price quotes available before we run it through insurance
08, Looking ahead

Compliance & Monitoring Programs

Beyond just filling your prescription, we keep an eye on patterns, refill timing, interactions with new meds, gaps in therapy, and we check in when something looks off. Catching a missed refill or a dosing issue early prevents the bigger problems that send people to the ER.

  • Periodic check-ins for patients on chronic medications
  • Regular drug-interaction screening as your regimen changes
  • Communication with your prescriber when adjustments may help
  • Available for caregivers managing a loved one's medications
